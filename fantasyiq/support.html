Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Sleeper league?

Go to Settings, tap "Leagues", then "Manage Connection". Enter your Sleeper username and select your league.

Why are projections showing 0.0?

Projections update during the NFL season. In the offseason, some data sources may not have current projections available.

How do I switch between leagues?

Tap the league name in the top-right corner of any tab, or go to Settings and tap "Leagues" to see all your connected leagues.

Does the app collect my data?

No. Fantasy IQ Companion does not collect, share, or sell any personal data. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What scoring formats are supported?

PPR, Half PPR, and Standard scoring formats are supported. You can change the format in Settings or per-tab using the format picker.

How do I set up the widget?

Long-press your home screen, tap the "+" button, search for "Fantasy IQ", and add the widget. It shows your matchup projection and lineup verdicts.
